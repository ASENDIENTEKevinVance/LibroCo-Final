Year: 2000
Genre: Romance

London, 1813. Daphne Bridgerton is keen to marry and start a family, but to date, all of the men she has met have regarded her as a friend and no more. Simon Bassett, Duke of Hastings has quite the opposite problem: he is regarded as the most eligible bachelor but desires nothing more than to remain single and childless. When Simon meets Daphne and learns of her troubles, he devises a plan that he believes will suit them both: they will feign a courtship.
